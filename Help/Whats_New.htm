What's New

 08/04/2003

Link to Archimedes

The Archimedes system contains the corporate structure for all Labels along with the accounting information associated to each Label, including Operating Company, Super Label, and Sub Label codes.  Archimedes also contains the Artist roster associated to each Label.  And, finally Archimedes holds all Project IDs that have been associated to an Artist and ultimately attached to a Label.  

 

The Archimedes to Milestone interface enables you to select a project from the new Project Search screen.  The advantage of this interface is that many fields you previously entered manually will now be automatically populated on the Selection screen.

Process Changes to the Selection screen

  1. The NEW button on the Physical and Digital selection screens no longer brings up a new Selection screen; the NEW button will activate the Project Search screen.  On this screen you will enter criteria to search the Archimedes database for Project related information for a Digital or Physical product.

  2. The REVISE button on the Physical and Digital Selection screens allows you to re-select a new Project for your existing Product.  Clicking the REVISE button will activate the Project Search screen.  

  3. The COPY DIGITAL button on the Physical Selection screen will assist you to copy information from a Physical Product to a new Digital Product.  

New  Terms within Milestone – Releasing Family, Imprint, Encryption, Product Type

  1. Releasing Family: This field replaces Family.  It denotes the Label Group releasing the product.  In most cases, Milestone will populate this field automatically.  

  2. Imprint: This field denotes the Label logo associated to an Artist that is to appear on the product.  In most cases it is the same as the Corporate Financial Label.  This field may be overwritten on the Selection screen for a particular Product, if the logo on that Product differs from the Imprint that is normally associated to that Artist.  This field can also be changed in Archimedes, if the logo associated to that Artist on all cases should be changed.  Making this change in Archimedes will cause Milestone to display a new Imprint Label on all subsequent products for this Artist.  Imprint appears on both the Physical and Digital Selection screens.

  3. Encryption: This field denotes whether a physical CD is to have copyright protection encoding (i.e. Cactus).  This field displays on the Physical PFM Form only.  The default is N.  Change the value to “Y” if the product is encrypted.

  4. Product Type:  The Physical, Digital, and All hyperlinks at the bottom of the Notepad limit the display by product type with All being the default.  The Product Type radio buttons on the Notepad Search limit the search results by product type with All being the default.  

Ability to track Digital Products and generate Reports

You can now enter and track Commercial Digital Products in Milestone and select specific Schedules for Digital tasks.  The Digital Product screens include Schedule, Selection, and PFM Form.

How can you tell a Physical Product screen from a Digital Product screen

  1. Physical Product screens have a blue background. Physical Product screens include Schedule, Selection, Manufacturing, PFM Form, and BOM Form.

  2. Digital Product screens have a rose background. Digital Product screens include Schedule, Selection, and PFM Form.

  3. The Reports screen is common to both Physical and Digital Product and continues to have the blue background.

New templates for Digital Products

  1. Prior to Physical - Select this Digital Schedule Type when the Digital product is to be released before the corresponding Physical Product.

  2. Simultaneous to Physical - Select this Digital Schedule Type when the Digital product is to be released "on the same day and date" or after the street date of the corresponding Physical Product.

  3. Digital Only - Select this Digital Schedule Type when the Digital product is not an exact duplicate of an existing or upcoming Physical product.

  4. Artist Session - Select this Digital Schedule Type when the Artist will be recording tracks specifically for Digital release with one business partner.  

 New PFM for Digital Products

The PFM Form for Digital Products holds many of the same fields as the Physical PFM Form.  A great deal of these fields are read-only, therefore there are not as many fields to complete.

New Report for Digital Products

Select ENT - Digital Production Schedule (LBL) to report on Digital Products for Label tasks
