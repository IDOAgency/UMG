Getting to the Selection Screen

Before you can review, create and/or edit Selection screens, you must first go to it. Keep in mind you must locate your release in the Notepad before you review or edit it. Follow these steps:

1.      By default, the Labels tab is activated when you log in to Milestone. So, normally, you can skip this step. However, if you’ve clicked into a different tab, such as Admin, click the Labels tab at the top of the screen. The Labels tab is highlighted.

2.      Click Selection in the menu bar. Selection screen appears.

3.      If you’re editing an existing product, locate the product with which you wish to work in the Notepad. To do this, either navigate to or search for the appropriate title.

See also

See Using Navigation Buttons to Locate Selections or Searching for Selections in the Basic Training section of this manual.

Understanding the Selection screen

The appearance of the Selection screen changes based on the content of the screen and the way your ID has been setup. Keep these in mind as you work:

·        Your access to products is determined by your user setup. That is, your access is limited to only those Releasing Families linked to your ID.

·        You can be given View access to certain families, Edit access to others. In this situation, you don’t see the write function buttons – New, Revise, Edit, Copy, Copy Digital and Delete – unless you have edit rights to the Releasing Family for the current product. These buttons are discussed in greater detail throughout this chapter.

·        For physical products, the Selection screen appears with a blue background.

·        For digital products, the Selection screen appears with a pink background. Only fields associated with digital products are shown.
