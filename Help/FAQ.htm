FAQ

How do I close a Project in Milestone?

A project can not be closed until all the tasks the schedule are completed, including UML task. After all tasks have been completed, either change the the status on the Selection screen to "Close" or, select the close button from the Schedule screen.

 

How do I apply a Template if Milestone does not suggest one?

Click on the word "search" next to "Select a Template".

  • Conduct a search based upon name, configuration or product line. A listing of templates will appear below the summarized selection information.

  • To select a template, click in the option button to the left of the template name. The tasks will display under the selection information.

  • Click on the Save button located just under the menu bar (or the Save button on the bottom of the screen) to apply the template to this release.

 

How do I get a sell code, BOM or PFM values added to Milestone?

Send an email to your CLO. After the request has been approved, the item will be added to Milestone.
