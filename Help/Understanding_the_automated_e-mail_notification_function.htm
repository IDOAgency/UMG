Understanding the automated e-mail notification function

PFM notifications are sent as PDF files attached to e-mails, as shown in this example. With a few exceptions (Local Product Number and related fields), the data on this form is then entered manually (as of June, 2003) into the Legacy system.

Regarding the e-mail notification function, keep in mind the following points:

·        For forms with the Final button clicked, the Save button changes to Save+Send.

·        The first time Save+Send is clicked, the e-mail notification is sent in Add mode.

·        Each subsequent time Save+Send is clicked, e-mail notifications are sent in Change mode  and the version number in the # field is incremented. For this reason, it’s important to use the Final button judiciously.

Note: 

An Email Notification pop-up box also displays within the Selection screen when a change has been made to a field on the Selection screen that affects a field on a PFM form in Final status.
