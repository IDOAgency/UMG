Editing Task on a Schedule

After tasks are assigned, the user has the option of editing the dates calculated by Milestone. Task dates can be changed on an individual basis by deleting and typing in a new date. When a due date is changed, the weeks to release field is deleted for that task. This reduces the possibility of re-calculating a changed due date in the schedule.

Updating an Existing Schedule

As production of a release progresses, the completion date and status should be updated for each task. UML will be responsible for updating tasks that are specific to UML.

Wks (Weeks or Days to Release)

The week or days to release field can be changed. To change the days to release, a number or the letter "D" followed by a number can be entered into this field. The weeks to release can be changed by entering the letter of the day (M,T,W,TH,F) followed by the number of weeks into this field. To calculate the new due date after changing this field, the "RECAL" button must be selected after saving the changes.

Completion and Due Dates

Completion or Due Dates can be entered into the schedule by entering a date using slashes (1/1/00) dashes (1-1-00) or dots (1.1.00) into the date field. Dates can also be entered into these fields by using the pop-up calendar. To activate the pop-up calendar, double click on the date field.

  • If the due date is past today's date, the background of the due date field will be pink.

  • If the completion date is past the due date, the background of the completion date field will be pink.

  • Completion dates can not be entered that are 7 days beyond today's date.

Task Comments

Task comments are entered into Milestone by double clicking the icon to the right of the completion date field. After a task comment has been saved, a pencil will appear in the icon.

  • Comments can only be entered for a task for which the user has edit rights assigned.

Status

The status drop down is an optional field. The 'Hold' and 'Pend' status are display only.

  • When the 'Done' status is selected, a dummy date of 9/9/99 will be entered into the completion date field when the record is saved, (unless a completion date has been entered).

  • If "N/A" (not applicable) is selected a dummy date of 9/9/99 will be entered into the completion date field when the record is saved.

  • If the status 'Today' is selected, the date the status is selected will be entered into the completion date field when the record is saved, (unless a completion date has been entered).

Vendor

The vendor field is optional. It can be used to enter a vendor name for a task or a contact name. The vendor's or contact's name is entered in this field by typing in the vendor box. They can also be entered by double clicking the vendor field and entering the name into the pop-up text box.
