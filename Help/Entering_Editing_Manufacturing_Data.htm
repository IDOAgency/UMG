Entering/Editing Manufacturing Data

For UML production users only: Follow these steps to enter and/or edit data on the Manufacturing screen:

10.  Click Manufacturing from the menu bar. 

11.  Using the Notepad, scroll to or search for the product for which you wish to enter or edit Manufacturing data.

12.  Click once on the selection to select (highlight) it. Entries from the Selection form automatically populate the appropriate fields on the Manufacturing form.

Keep in mind…

Entries in fields – including comments popups – from the Selection  screen are read-only. Changes made to them must be made in the Selection screen by the appropriate label users.

13.  Pulldown the Distribution list. Choose Deep or Hits. This entry determines the depots from which sales orders are filled, once product is available for sale.

14.  Pulldown the UML Contact list and select the appropriate contact name for your product.

Adding vendor information

15.  Click the Add Vendor button. A new line is added for vendor information at the bottom of this form.

16.  Use the pulldown to specify Vendor/Plant name.

17.  Go to the PO Qty field. Enter requested quantity.

18.  Go to the Completed Qty field, if needed. Enter quantity actually manufactured.

19.   Repeat the process, Adding Vendor Information, until all vendors have been entered.

20.  Once all entries have been made, click the Save button to save your Manufacturing form.
