Generating temporary product numbers

Temporary product numbers act as placeholders, allowing you to save products until you’re able to generate valid local product numbers. You might, for example, use a temporary number when you’re entering Selection information for a product that doesn’t yet have a corresponding Archimedes project ID. Follow these steps.

1.      Click the Temp Product # button. A temporary product number is generated automatically, the Selection screen is saved and the Schedule screen appears.

Keep in mind…

·        Temporary product numbers are formatted beginning with TEMP,  followed by a sequential number (TEMPnnnnnn). This number is overwritten when you generate a Local Product Number using the Generate Local Product # button.

·        If no entry is made in the Title ID field, and a temporary product number is generated, the temporary number (TEMPnnnnnn) is copied automatically into the Title ID field.

·        All required fields – those with red asterisks – must be completed before you can generate temporary product numbers. A dialog box lists missing fields if you attempt to do one before the other
