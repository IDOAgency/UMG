Maintaining Schedules

Maintaining schedules in Milestone involves entering completion dates and comments as needed. Keep in mind Label personnel are responsible for updating the tasks relating to their activities. Likewise, UML or eCommerce personnel are responsible for updating their own tasks.

Maintaining schedules involves these general steps:

·        Entering/updating task completion dates

·        Entering comments about tasks

·        Updating task status entries (optional)

·        Entering/updating vendor or contact names relevant to tasks (optional)

These steps are described in detail in the sections that follow.

Entering task completion dates

Follow these steps to enter completion dates:

1.      Move your cursor to the Completion date field you wish to enter.

2.      Delete the existing entry, if needed.

3.      Enter a new due date using any of the following formats:

·        mm/dd/yy

·        mm-dd-yy

·        mm.dd.yy

You can also use the handy pop-up calendar to enter dates. To do this, double click on the date field and the calendar pop-up appears.

Use the scroll buttons to move to the month and/or year of the date you wish to use.

Click once on the date you wish to use. The schedule screen returns, the date you specify appears in the date field.

4.      Click Save to save your changes.

Keep in mind…

·        If the completion date is past the due date, the background turns pink.

·        Completion dates cannot be entered.

Entering comments about tasks

Use the comments popup window to enter supplemental information about tasks. Follow these steps:

1.      Click the blank page icon to the right of the completion date field. The Comments popup window appears.

2.      Enter comments as needed in the text box.

3.      Do one of the following:

·        Click Save Comments to save your entry and return. A pencil is added to the icon to indicate comments have been entered.

·        Click Cancel to return without saving.

4.      Click Save to save your changes.

Updating status entries (optional)

Update the Status field, if needed, to reflect changes in tasks. Follow these steps:

1.      Pull down the Status list for the task you wish to update.

2.      Choose the appropriate selection (see below for details about available options).

3.      Click Save to save your changes.

Keep in mind…

·        Hold and Pend (pending) status entries are display only. That is, no processing is halted or delayed.

·        When you select Done, a dummy date of 9/9/99 is entered in the completion date field when the record is saved (unless a completion date has been entered previously).

·        If N/A (not applicable) is selected, a dummy date of 9/9/99 is entered in the completion date field when the record is saved.

·        If Today is selected, the current date is entered in the completion date field when the record is saved, (unless a completion date has been entered previously).

Entering vendor or contact names

Use the Vendor field to store names of vendors or contacts as needed. Follow these steps.

1.      Move cursor to Vendor field of task you wish to update.

2.      Do one of the following:

·        Click once to move cursor into field. Enter text.

·        Double click in the field. A popup text box appears. Enter text. Click Save to save your entry. Click Cancel to return to the previous window.

3.      Click Save in the Schedule screen to save your changes.
