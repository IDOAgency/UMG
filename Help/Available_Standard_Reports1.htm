Available Standard Reports

Milestone reports can be configured and customized using a wide range of criteria and specifications. Three basic types of reports are available.

·        Enterprise reports are generic reports for physical products which can be accessed and run by all labels. You can recognize these reports by the ENT designation at the beginning of their titles (example: ENT: Commercial Release Schedule). These reports are listed below along with a brief description.

·        Digital reports are Enterprisereports which have been designed to include data only for digital products. As of this writing (August, 2003), two digital reports have been created, one for labels, the other for eCommerce. These reports also are listed and described below.

·        Custom reports are specific to labels. These reports meet a variety of specific requirements for individual labels.  Because of the number and variety of these reports, they are neither listed nor described here.

All these reports feature standard output formats. That is, you can’t change report columns and the way they appear on reports. However, the data contained within reports can be filtered and tailored to your specific requirements.

Some of the more commonly used Enterprisereports are listed here. Keep in mind this is not a complete list, many Enterprisereports are omitted, as are all label-specific (custom) reports.

ENT-Commercial Release Schedule

The ENT-Commercial Release Schedule lists commercial releases sorted by family and company, and provides summary information for each release. 

ENT-Release Schedule

The ENT-Release Schedule lists product releases sorted by family and company, and provides summary information, including the Comments field, for each release.

ENT-Release Schedule by Label

The ENT-Release Schedule by Label lists product releases by label and provides summary information, including comments, for each release. This report can be filtered by label using the pulldown list supplied. Report outputs are sorted by label, then by Full Length and Single configurations. 

ENT-Production Status

The ENT-Production Status report provides schedule details for specified product releases, sorted by family, company, division, label, artist and street/ship date.

ENT-Past Due Releases

The ENT-Past Due Releases report provides information about products for a given company and the status (complete, past due and so on) of the tasks associated with their release.

ENT-To Do List

The ENT-To Do List report lists tasks not completed based on criteria you specify.

ENT-Task Due by Title ID

The ENT-Task Due by Title ID report lists tasks due and groups them by Title ID.

ENT-Deadline Schedule

The ENT-Deadline Schedule report calculates and lists due dates for tasks and templates based on your specifications. Reports are sorted by templates, and include columns for all street dates within your specified range.

ENT-Releases Without Schedules

The ENT-Releases Without Schedules report lists all products in Milestone for which no schedules have been assigned.
