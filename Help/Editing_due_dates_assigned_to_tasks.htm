Editing due dates assigned to tasks

You can the edit system-calculated due dates assigned to tasks as needed. Use one of these methods:

·        Delete and enter new (and specific) due dates. When you do this, the weeks to release entry for these tasks are cleared, to reduce confusion and the possibility of recalculating a changed date.

·        Change the weeks or days to release entries, then use the Recalc button to recalculate all due dates.

Procedures for both methods are given below.

Entering new due dates

Follow these steps to change due dates assigned to tasks:

1.      Move your cursor to the due date you wish to change.

2.      Delete the existing entry.

3.      Enter a new due date using any of the following formats:

·        mm/dd/yy

·        mm-dd-yy

·        mm.dd.yy

You can also use the handy pop-up calendar to enter dates. To do this, double click on the date field and the calendar pop-up appears.

Use the scroll buttons to move to the month and/or year of the date you wish to use.

Click once on the date you wish to use. The schedule screen returns, the date you specify appears in the Due date field.

Keep in mind….

If the due date is past today’s date, the background of the due date field turns pink.

Changing weeks/days to release entries

Follow these steps to change weeks or days to release and recalculate due dates:

1.      To clear all entries in the Wks column (optional) click the Clear button. A confirmation dialog appears. Do one of the following:

·        Click OK to clear all entries. The Schedule screen returns. All editable fields in the Wks and Due columns are blank.

·        Click Cancel to return to previous display without making any changes.

2.      Enter new weeks or days to release in the Wks column for all tasks you wish to change. Use the format NN 99, where n represents the day of the week (M, T, W, TH, F) and 99 represents the number of weeks before release.

3.      Click Save. The screen refreshes.

4.      Click Recalc. A confirmation dialog (shown) appears. Do one of the following:

·        Click Incomplete Tasks Only to recalculate only due dates assigned to tasks for which no completed dates have been entered.

·        Click All Tasks to recalculate due dates for all tasks, regardless of whether completed dates have been entered. Your completed dates are not cleared when you use this option.

·        Click Cancel to return to the previous screen without recalculating any dates.

5.      Click Save to save your changes.
