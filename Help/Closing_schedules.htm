Closing schedules

Product status can be changed to closed once all tasks assigned to the product have a completion date. Before you can do this, all tasks – label, UML and eCommerce tasks – must have a completion date entered or status changed to either Done or N/A.

Closing schedules changes the product status to Closed and removes products from regular Notepad views. To view closed products in the Notepad you must search with the Include Closed/Cancel checkbox enabled.

Follow these steps:

1.      Click the Close button, at the top of the task list. The screen refreshes and status changes to Closed. If the above requirements have not been met, a message is displayed.
