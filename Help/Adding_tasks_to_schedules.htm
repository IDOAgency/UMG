Adding tasks to schedules

In the section above, procedures are given for bringing entire sets of tasks into the Schedule screen. Once this has been done, you can add individual tasks, as needed. Follow the steps listed below.

Assigning Individual Tasks

1.      Click the Switch to Tasks button at the bottom of the Notepad screen. The screen refreshes and the Notepad now lists pre-defined tasks.

2.      Use the navigation buttons to scroll to the task you wish to use. Or click the Search button and search for specific tasks.

3.      Click the checkbox next to the task you wish to apply. You can click multiple tasks on the same page, but once you move to another page, your checkbox entries are lost.

4.      Click the Assign Tasks button to bring the tasks you select into the current schedule. When you do, those tasks are removed from the Notepad to prevent adding duplicate tasks.

5.      Click Save in the Selection screen to save your changes.
