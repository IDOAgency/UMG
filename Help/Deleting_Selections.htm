Deleting Selections

1.      To delete a Selection record, click the DELETE button. A confirmation dialog, shown left, appears.

2.      Click OK to delete the current record. Click Cancel to go back to the Selection screen.

IMPORTANT

You cannot delete Selection records for products that have schedules assigned to them. Be sure to delete Schedule information before you delete Selection records. See Milestone Schedules, in another chapter of this manual, for details.
