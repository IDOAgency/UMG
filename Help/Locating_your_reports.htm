Locating your reports

Use the Notepad to find the standard report you wish to customize and run. Follow these steps.

1.      From the Labels tab, click Reports on the menu bar. The screen refreshes, the Notepad now lists available standard reports. Available criteria for the current report appears at right.

2.      Use the navigation buttons or the Search button/function to locate the report you wish to run.

3.      Click once on the report title in the Notepad to select (highlight) it. Report criteria for this report now appear at right.
