Assinging Individual Tasks

At the bottom of the search results section of the screen (left-hand side of the screen), click on "Switch to Tasks". This will open a results listing on the left-hand side of the screen, displaying all available tasks.

Based upon the number of tasks displayed in the results listing, the user may need to click on the word "next" to display additional tasks. Likewise, click on "prev" (previous) to review previously displayed tasks. Or, a search can be conducted to locate a particular task.

To select a task, click in the box to the left of the task Name.

Click on the "Assign Tasks" button at the bottom of the results listing section. The task will display just under the summarized selection information.

Once a task is assigned to the schedule, it is removed from the task listing on the left-hand side of the screen in order to avoid duplication of tasks.

Click on the Save button located just under the menu bar.
