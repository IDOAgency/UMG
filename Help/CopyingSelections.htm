Copying Selections

In the Milestone Selection screen, you can copy:

·        existing physical products to new, physical products

·        existing physical products to new, digital products

·        existing digital products to new, digital products.

Keep in mind you cannot copy digital products to physical products.

Follow these steps to copy products:

1.      Go to the product record, either physical or digital, in the Notepad. Click once on the title to select (highlight) it. See Searching for Selections in the Basic Training chapter of this manual for details.

2.      Do one of the following:

In physical selection screens:

·        Click Copy to copy the current screen to a new, physical selection screen.

·        Click Copy Digital to copy the current screen to a digital selection screen.

In digital selection screens:

·        Click Copy to copy the current screen to a new, digital selection screen.

3.      A new Selection screen appears, either physical or digital, depending on the previous step.

4.      Make changes to fields as needed. Be sure all fields with red asterisks (*) have entries before you save.

5.      Enter the Prefix/Local Product # information as needed.

6.      For physical selections you can generate local or temporary product numbers, as needed, by clicking the appropriate button at bottom. Keep in mind all fields with blue asterisks (*) must be entered before you can generate Local Product Numbers.

7.      Click Save.

Note

If the Project ID for the existing product does not exist in the Archimedes database, a message displays and you’re prevented from copying unless you revise the product’s Project ID. As an alternative, you can click the New button and create it from the Project Search module as discussed previously.
