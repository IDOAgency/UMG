Understanding the automated BOM e-mail notification function

Keep in mind the following points:

·        For forms with the Final button clicked, the Save button changes to Save+Send.

·        Every time Save-Send is clicked, an e-mail notification is sent. The first e-mail is sent in Add mode, subsequent e-mails are sent in Change mode and the version number in the # field is incremented. These e-mails describe what has taken place and include a PDF version of the BOM. Changes. To avoid repetitive and superfluous e-mail notifications, use discretion when clicking the Final button.

Note: 

An Email Notification pop-up box also displays within the Selection screen when a change has been made to a field on the Selection screen that affects a field on a BOM form in Final status.
